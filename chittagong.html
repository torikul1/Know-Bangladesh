Chittagong Division

In 1947 the area was continued as a Division, as one of four divisions of East Bengal. The upland districts of Khagrachhari, Rangamati and Bandarban together comprise an area known as the Chittagong Hill Tracts. Before 1995, the four districts of Sylhet Division were also a part of Chittagong Division. It covers the most southern areas of the country and is also the second largest one.

Chittagong Has This Number Of Districts :

  1. Chittagong
  2. Bandarban
  3. Brahmanbaria
  4. Chandpur
  5. Comilla
  6. Cox's Bazar
  7. Feni
  8. Khagrachari
  9. Lakshmipur
  10. Noakhali
  11. Rangamati
